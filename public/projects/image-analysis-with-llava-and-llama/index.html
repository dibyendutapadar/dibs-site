The Tech That Makes It All Happen#

Large Language Models (LLMs)#

At the heart of this magic is obviously LLMs (Large Language Models). LLMs have been game-changers in making our interactions with technology more natural, almost like chatting with a super-smart friend.

LLaMA and LLAVA#

Now, let‚Äôs talk about two key players: LLaMA (Large Language Model Accelerated) and LLAVA (Large Language Vision Agent). LLaMA is one of your go-to LLM for processing text, while LLaVA is a novel end-to-end trained large multimodal model that combines a vision encoder and Vicuna for general-purpose visual and language understanding.

Together, they‚Äôre the dynamic duo that enables this app to seamlessly juggle both text and images.

I have used 8b models of both, but if you have access to superior computing power (read GPU) you can use larger models as well

  1. Upload Your Photos: Start by uploading your images‚Äîwhether they‚Äôre in PNG, JPG, or JPEG format.
